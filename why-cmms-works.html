MeetMaxpanda CMMS

Businesses that fail to minimize operational costs can't be competitive and rarely stay in business. Businesses using a CMMS such as Maxpanda to manage their maintenance operations keep them strong and viable for producing quality goods and services year after year. Most businesss (even governments) are dependent on physical assets to produce a product or service. For instance a pizza restaurant is dependent on ovens and delivery vehicles. The organization’s equipment (assets) needs electricity, people, water and natural gas in order to function at 100% capacity.

Energy


Can be saved by assuring assets function as efficiently as possible through these preventative schedules. Assets with long life-cycles can avoid costly replacement expenses that may occur each year.

Equipment


Whatever the mix of equipment, people and energy use, a business’s success depends on keeping assets working as much as possible with minimal downtime using properly scheduled maintenance procedures.
